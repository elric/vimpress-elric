* Commands :
============
 ":BlogList [count]"
   Lists recent <count> articles in the blog, default is 10, use 0 for all
 ":BlogNew"
   Opens page to write new article
 ":BlogOpen <id>"
   Opens the article <id> for edition
 ":BlogSend"
   Saves the article to the blog
 ":BlogSave"
   Saves the article as a draft
